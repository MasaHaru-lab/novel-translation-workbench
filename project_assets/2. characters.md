# Characters

## Confirmed / working renderings

### Qin Liuxi
- Chinese: 秦流西
- English rendering: Qin Liuxi
- Notes: Main character. Do not drift to "Qi Liuxi" or other variants.

### Old Lady Qin
- Chinese: 秦老太太
- English rendering: Old Lady Qin
- Notes: Senior matriarch figure in the Qin family.

### Lady Wang
- Chinese: 王氏
- English rendering: Lady Wang
- Notes: Use consistently. Do not alternate casually with Madam Wang unless a deliberate system change is made.

### Lady Xie
- Chinese: 谢氏
- English rendering: Lady Xie

### Ding Momo
- Chinese: 丁嬷嬷
- English rendering: Ding Momo
- Notes: Keep “Momo” for now rather than forcing “Nanny” or “Nurse.” Final treatment should stay aligned with the broader system for household titles and forms of address.

### Qin Yuanshan
- Chinese: 秦元山
- English rendering: Qin Yuanshan

### Lady Gu
- Chinese: 顾氏
- English rendering: Lady Gu
- Notes: Third branch wife of the Qin family. Carrying twins; labor induced by the shock of the raid.

### Qihuang
- Chinese: 岐黄
- English rendering: Qihuang
- Notes: Keep as "Qihuang" — transliteration for personal maid / attendant. Not a medical term in this context.

### Aunt Li
- Chinese: 李婶
- English rendering: Aunt Li
- Notes: Senior household servant / lead maid attached to major women such as Lady Wang, Lady Gu, or Old Lady Qin. Not a blood-relative "aunt" — in this book the form refers to a senior household servant role.

### Doctor Zhang / Zhang Dafu
- Chinese: 张大夫 / 张老大夫
- English rendering: Doctor Zhang
- Notes: Senior physician. Canonical rendering is "Doctor Zhang" — NOT "Dr. Zhang", NOT "Zhang Dafu". Keep stable within and across chapters. See unresolved_decisions for 大夫 address system.

### Zhang Zhuoliang
- Chinese: 张卓良
- English rendering: Zhang Zhuoliang
- Notes: Doctor Zhang's grandson. Keep as "Zhang Zhuoliang" — do not abbreviate or Westernize.

### Doctor Gao
- Chinese: 高大夫
- English rendering: Doctor Gao
- Notes: Canonical rendering is "Doctor Gao". Supplementary physician. Keep stable — do not switch to "Dr. Gao" or "Gao Dafu".

### Doctor Yang
- Chinese: 杨大夫
- English rendering: Doctor Yang
- Notes: Canonical rendering is "Doctor Yang". Supplementary physician. Keep stable — do not switch to "Dr. Yang" or "Yang Dafu".

### Chi Yuan Daoist
- Chinese: 赤元老道
- English rendering: Chi Yuan Daoist
- Notes: 赤=chi=red, rendered as pinyin here for name-like flow. Prefer this over "Red Yuan Daoist" (too descriptive) or "Daoist Red Yuan" (over-forced). Keep stable.
